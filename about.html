Nasiba Karimova

Tajik-born illustrator and graphic designer with a Fine Arts background, working in digital visual storytelling.

I tell stories through color and light. Women’s experiences, childhood memories, cultural details, and the quiet beauty of ordinary things inspire me deeply. I draw to capture warmth and honesty — to preserve the fleeting moments that feel like poetry.
